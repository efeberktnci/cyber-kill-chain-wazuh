# Reports

Cross-scenario reporting belongs here. Scenario-specific incident reports remain inside their scenario directories to preserve case context.

## Planned Contents

- detection coverage summaries
- cross-phase analyst observations
- false-positive and false-negative review notes
- final SOC report with unified timeline
- portfolio-grade executive summary once the attack sequence is complete

## Publishing Strategy

This directory will start filling after multiple primary attack phases are complete.

That sequencing is intentional:

- scenario directories hold the case-local evidence first
- `reports/` becomes valuable when there is enough completed material to compare across phases

The first major report artifact expected here is the final SOC-wide reporting package after the core attack sequence is published.
